Profile

Rintarou Suna is the middle blocker of Inarizaki High, recognized for his sharp reading ability and composed playstyle. Though often appearing aloof and uninterested, he is a reliable defender whose calm presence balances the team. After graduation, he becomes a professional player for EJP Raijin in Japan’s V.League.
